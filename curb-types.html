Curb Types
Leveraging publicly available data to measure the defining spatial qualities of curbs.
To summarize the different built environments that characterize Parisian curbs, we divide the city into 100x100-meter cells and combine data from the Paris Open Data portal, Google Street View images, and Google Places to compute measurements of the built environment for each cell.
Using Machine learning algorithms, we categorize each of the resulting 10,000 cells of Paris into one of six typologies, ranging from Urban Hotspots that have the most commercial activity to Calm Streets typical of residential environments.
